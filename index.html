About Me

Creating wealth is a priceless asset few people possess, in the 21st Century that skill will separate who survives and who thrives. This is the first time since WW2 upward financial mobility of the next generation is unlikely, western nations crippled under mountains of debt with citizens trapped in the post Bretton Woods era Ponzi scheme . The period from 1950 to 2020 was an aberration and now we return to a more uncertain, volatile period, a mean reversion of the human experience. Creating wealth today is about rising above the chaos to lift yourself and your future generations out of the vertically integrated hamster wheel of society. I have 40 years of success building startups with a library of knowledge on what to do and not to do. Feel free to contact me if you need help getting your startup pointed in the right direction.
